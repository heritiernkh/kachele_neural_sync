About NeuralSync Live

Built for the Gemini 3 Global Hackathon

Our Mission

NeuralSync Live transforms traditional learning into an adaptive, personalized experience. We believe education should adapt to the learner, not the other way around. By leveraging the multimodal capabilities of Gemini 3, we create a tutor that sees, hears, and understands context just like a human expert would.

Powered by Gemini 3

This platform showcases the incredible capabilities of Gemini 3's multimodal AI:

  • Real-time video and document analysis
  • Advanced reasoning for step-by-step guidance
  • Low-latency interactions for seamless learning
  • Adaptive difficulty and personalized feedback

The Team

Created with passion for the Gemini 3 Global Hackathon. We're pushing the boundaries of AI-powered education.
